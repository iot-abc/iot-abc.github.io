Welcome to IoT ABC to XYZ.

This site serves as the book-site for the book which we are currently working on. Our book is titled Learning IoT using Particle Photon and Electron. It will be released in January, 2016 and we are working hard to make the book an awesome read for first time IoT hackers as well as seasoned enthusiasts.

What will this site do?

We will be publishing our update on the book on this site which will help you track the progress. Additionally, we will add a blog about IoT mostly focussing on getting things to work and trouble-shooting. From the time we started working on the book, we realised that debugging hardware devices are very difficult and often finding the right documentation or step is difficult. We will also be posting videos, photos and texts about the projects we build in our free-time which will serve as starting point for you guys!

What do we hack on?

We work on number of devices ranging from resistors to Leap Motion. For the book, we are using Particle products; Photon, Core and Electron. We are working CylonJS to integrate everything around us to fulfil the vision of IoT or rather IoE (Internet of Everything). We love to hack and build awesome prototypes. Our technology stack starts from the low-level networking protocol CoAP, MQTT to all the way to Node JS. We <3 Angular JS and use mostly for building web dashboards. Our favourite database store has been MongoDB with Express as the server.
